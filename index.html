How to Play

In this grid, each cell can contain any number from 1 to 10. However, a number once used in a cell cannot be repeated within that cell's row and column. Failure to comply results in the loss of a life
The game comprises two primary modes: Campaign and Free Roam. The "Campaign" mode consists of four levels, and upon winning all of them, your name will be added to the leaderboard. In "Free Roam" mode, you have the freedom to select and play columns and rows as you desire.
